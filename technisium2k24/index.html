Technisium : Unveiling the Circuitry of Innovation"


Technisium, the flagship event of our technical branch, celebrates the marvels of circuitry, innovation, and technological prowess. With an aim to foster a culture of creativity and ingenuity, Technisium serves as a platform for budding engineers and technology enthusiasts to showcase their skills, exchange ideas, and push the boundaries of what's possible in the realm of circuitry. About Us

About SIT Tumkur

Siddaganga Institute of Technology (SIT), located in Tumkur and established in 1963, is part of the Sree Siddaganga Education Society, led by His Holiness Dr. Sree Sree Sivakumara Swamiji. SIT is renowned for its commitment to providing free education, food, and shelter to over 9000 underprivileged children. Situated across 50 lush green acres, SIT offers a serene learning environment akin to Bangalore's climate. Initially offering Civil, Electrical, and Mechanical Engineering branches with 120 students, SIT has expanded to 12 UG programs (950 students annually) and 16 PG programs (432 students annually). Recognized as research centers by Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belagavi, 16 departments offer Ph.D. and M.Sc. by Research programs. Currently, 204 research scholars are pursuing Ph.D., with 55 having completed their degrees.
